Practical activity: Emotional Reaction

Question: As a Project Manager, what might be your response to managing the emotional reactions of a customer?

Emotional reactions of customers can be gotten from using the User experience design(UX) which according to (Van der Linden, 2019) in the Components of User Experience model, examines; the subjective customer feelings and perceptions, which stem from the motor expressions that occur during usage. These Emotional reactions encompass and decide the user experience when using their experience design for a physical or digital product design; a business launch, website design or project design. Hence, it is one factor that should be anticipated during the designing process, so that the emotional reactions can be managed effectively and this is where project management comes into action.

According to Haji. R, (2018), Project Management is a system used to measure, track, plan, forecast or predict possible risks and reactions and manage all project activities in other to enhance project success and complete listed goals. A person who uses this system is called a project manager. When carrying out a User Experience design project, a project manager is highly effective and important for the project's success.

The central role of a project manager for User experience(UX); Managing, forecasting tracking, and improving customer reactions to User Experience. The customer reactions are the varying emotions the customer feels while using a product, these emotions could be progressive toward positive experience or regressive toward negative experience, depending on how it was anticipated by the project manager who then communicates this with the user experience designers.

Steffan, (2011) in her research on Customer Experience management discussed that managing customers' reactions should be done with the intent of creating a memorable experience for the customer and for this to be possible, the customer should be the centre focus when designing a user experience before the product, idea or technology that will be used.

Therefore, as a project manager called to manage the reactions of customers or users; prioritizing and fully integrating the customer into projects and treating them as internal components of a user experience design will play a huge role in managing their reactions and getting emotional progression to a positive experience.
