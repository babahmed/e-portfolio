ANALYSIS OF INFORMATION GATHERED

There was several information encountered during this module. While a lot of contribution also has their weight, I find the post by Hung Wei Lin particularly interesting and the follow up by Alberto Rossotto and Yin Pai forming the basis of evaluating the situation surrounding NYSE Retail Liquidity Program software failure. Analysis from the different view of each commenter gave me a deeper understanding of the how system failure could be as a result of the negligence of the organization to adhere to a proper system implementation process. Sathira Padukka pointed that this could possibly have been avoided by having a proper and adequate testing in place. Other posts by participants such as the 737 Max by Alberto Rossotto, A train collision by Lin Pai, basically points to the same solution to adequate and rigorous testing of applications and software.

Initial Post

The initial post further explores scenarios where system failure has extended effect on all dependencies, and I was able to understand the impact of possible system failure on the society at large and not just the organization involved. A review at other peer contributions gives insight into how systems were restored after a failure and approaches taken to prevent further failure in response to the facilitators comment.
